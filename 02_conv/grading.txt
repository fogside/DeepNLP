Short info on grading rules:


- Preprocessing and tokenization - +2 pts
- Your NN is implemented (compiles) - +2 pts
- Your NN gets at least 0.65 AUC - +2 pts
- Your NN gets "ok" in both accuracy, auc and ap@k - +2 pts
- +0.5 point for each "good", extra +0.5 for each "great" (sum of pounts is rounded up)

Some ways to get bonus score
- adapt solution for kaggle competition (add screen with final score)
- do something more funny with embeddings (init with word2vec or pre-train or something)
- super-fast NN
